#Write-ChocolateySuccess

`Write-ChocolateySuccess $packageName`  
  
##Description
Notes a successful chocolatey install.    
     
##Parameters
###$packageName
This is an arbitrary name.  
Example: `'7zip'`  
  
##Examples
`Write-ChocolateySuccess 'StExBar'`
